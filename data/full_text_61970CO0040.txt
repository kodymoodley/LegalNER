PRELIMINARY QUESTIONS - REFERENCE TO THE COURT - APPRAISAL BY NATIONAL COURT - REQUEST BY THE PARTIES TO THE MAIN PROCEEDINGS FOR THE INTERPRETATION OF A PRELIMINARY RULING - INADMISSIBILITY  ( EEC TREATY , ART . ; STATUTE OF THE COURT OF JUSTICE OF THE EEC , ART . ; RULES OF PROCEDURE , ART . )   
Summary
ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY MAKES PROVISION FOR DIRECT CO-OPERATION BETWEEN THE COURT OF JUSTICE AND THE NATIONAL COURTS BASED ON A NON-CONTENTIOUS PROCEDURE IRRESPECTIVE OF ANY STEPS TAKEN BY THE PARTIES TO THE PROCEEDINGS AND IN THE COURSE OF WHICH SUCH PARTIES ARE MERELY INVITED TO SUBMIT OBSERVATIONS WITHIN THE LEGAL FRAMEWORK SET OUT BY THE COURT MAKING THE REFERENCE .  SINCE , WITHIN THE LIMITS ESTABLISHED BY ARTICLE , IT IS THUS FOR THE NATIONAL COURTS ALONE TO DECIDE ON THE PRINCIPLE AND PURPOSE OF ANY REFERENCE TO THE COURT OF JUSTICE IT FOLLOWS THAT IT IS ALSO THE TASK OF SUCH COURTS ALONE TO APPRAISE WHETHER THEY HAVE OBTAINED SUFFICIENT GUIDANCE FROM THE PRELIMINARY RULING DELIVERED IN RESPONSE TO THEIR QUESTION OR TO THE QUESTION OF A LOWER COURT OR WHETHER IT APPEARS TO THEM NECESSARY TO REFER THE MATTER ONCE MORE TO THE COURT OF JUSTICE . ACCORDINGLY THE PARTIES TO THE MAIN ACTION CANNOT RELY ON ARTICLE OF THE PROTOCOL ON THE STATUTE OF THE COURT OF JUSTICE OF THE EEC OR ON ARTICLE OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE IN ORDER TO REQUEST THE COURT TO INTERPRET JUDGMENTS DELIVERED IN PURSUANCE OF THE SAID ARTICLE .

AND  EDA S.R.L . AND OTHERS  BY WAY OF A PRELIMINARY RULING ON THE INTERPRETATION OF ARTICLES AND OF THE EEC TREATY ,   
Grounds
THE UNDERTAKING NOVIMPEX S.R.L ., WHICH IS IN THE COURSE OF WINDING UP BY THE COURT , HAS APPLIED TO THE COURT OF JUSTICE PURSUANT TO ARTICLE OF THE PROTOCOL ON THE STATUTE OF THE COURT OF JUSTICE AND TO ARTICLE OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE FOR AN INTERPRETATION OF THE JUDGMENT DELIVERED ON FEBRUARY (( ) ECR ) IN CASE 40/ON A REFERENCE FROM THE TRIBUNALE CIVILE E PENALE , MILAN , CONCERNING AN ACTION BETWEEN THE UNDERTAKING SIRENA AND OTHER PARTIES INCLUDING NOVIMPEX .  NOVIMPEX STATES THAT IT HAS BECOME NECESSARY TO OBTAIN AN INTERPRETATION OF THE JUDGMENT IN CASE 40/IN VIEW OF THE SUBSEQUENT DEVELOPMENT OF THE CASE-LAW OF THE COURT OF JUSTICE CONCERNING THE EXERCISE OF RIGHTS OF INDUSTRIAL AND COMMERCIAL PROPERTY EMBODIED IN THE JUDGMENTS IN CASE 192/, HAG , OF JULY (( ) ECR ), CASE 15/, CENTRAFARM , OF OCTOBER (( ) ECR ) AND CASE 119/, TERRAPIN V TERRANOVA , OF JUNE (( ) ECR ), A DEVELOPMENT WHICH HAS BEEN POINTED OUT BY THE ITALIAN COURTS DEALING WITH THE CASE AFTER DELIVERY OF THE JUDGMENT , FOLLOWING THE JUDGMENT OF THE COURT OF JUSTICE IN CASE 40/, BY THE TRIBUNALE CIVILE E PENALE , MILAN , IN THE SIRENA CASE . NOVIMPEX IS IN FACT REQUESTING THE COURT TO CLARIFY THE POSITION IN AN INTERPRETING JUDGMENT .  IN CONNEXION WITH THIS APPLICATION IT SHOULD BE NOTED THAT ARTICLE OF THE EEC TREATY MAKES PROVISION FOR DIRECT CO-OPERATION BETWEEN THE COURT OF JUSTICE AND THE NATIONAL COURTS BASED ON A NON-CONTENTIOUS PROCEDURE IRRESPECTIVE OF ANY STEPS TAKEN BY THE PARTIES TO THE PROCEEDINGS AND IN THE COURSE OF WHICH SUCH PARTIES ARE MERELY INVITED TO SUBMIT OBSERVATIONS WITHIN THE LEGAL FRAMEWORK SET OUT BY THE COURT MAKING THE REFERENCE .  SINCE , WITHIN THE LIMITS ESTABLISHED BY ARTICLE , IT IS THUS FOR THE NATIONAL COURTS ALONE TO DECIDE ON THE PRINCIPLE AND PURPOSE OF ANY REFERENCE TO THE COURT OF JUSTICE IT FOLLOWS THAT IT IS ALSO THE TASK OF SUCH COURTS ALONE TO     APPRAISE WHETHER THEY HAVE OBTAINED SUFFICIENT GUIDANCE FROM THE PRELIMINARY RULING DELIVERED IN RESPONSE TO THEIR QUESTION OR TO THE QUESTION OF A LOWER COURT OR WHETHER IT APPEARS TO THEM NECESSARY TO REFER THE MATTER ONCE MORE TO THE COURT OF JUSTICE . ACCORDINGLY THE PARTIES TO THE MAIN ACTION CANNOT RELY ON ARTICLE OF THE PROTOCOL ON THE STATUTE OF THE COURT OF JUSTICE OR ON ARTICLE OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE IN ORDER TO REQUEST THE COURT TO INTERPRET JUDGMENTS DELIVERED IN PURSUANCE OF THE SAID ARTICLE .   IN THOSE CIRCUMSTANCES THE APPLICATION SUBMITTED ON AUGUST BY NOVIMPEX MUST BE DISMISSED BY THE COURT OF ITS OWN MOTION AS INADMISSIBLE .
