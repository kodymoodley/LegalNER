++++HAVING REGARD TO THE APPLICATION SUBMITTED BY THE GOVERNMENT OF THE LAND OF NORTH RHINE - WESTPHALIA ON APRIL TO INTERVENE IN THE ACTION PENDING BEFORE THE COURT OF JUSTICE OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES,  ( ) 'GEITLING' RUHRKOHLEN-VERKAUFSGESELLSCHAFT MBH, ESSEN,  ( ) 'MAUSEGATT' RUHRKOHLEN-VERKAUFSGESELLSCHAFT MBH, ESSEN,  ( ) 'PRASIDENT' RUHRKOHLEN-VERKAUFSGESELLSCHAFT MBH, ESSEN,  ( ) THE MINING COMPANIES OF THE RUHR BASIN, GROUPED INTO AND REPRESENTED BY THE ABOVE THREE MARKETING COMPANIES,  V  HIGH AUTHORITY OF THE EUROPEAN COAL AND STEEL COMMUNITY,  
Grounds
HAVING REGARD TO THE WRITTEN OBSERVATIONS SUBMITTED BY THE PARTIES TO THE ORIGINAL CASE;  WHEREAS THE GOVERNMENT OF THE LAND OF NORTH RHINE-WESTPHALIA CLAIMS THAT IT HAS AN INTEREST IN THE RESULT OF THE ORIGINAL CASE SUBMITTED TO THE COURT AND SUBSTANTIATES ITS CLAIM WITH A PERTINENT STATEMENT OF THE REASONS GIVING RISE TO THAT INTEREST;  WHEREAS THE LAND OF NORTH RHINE-WESTPHALIA HAS ADEQUATELY SUBSTANTIATED ITS INTEREST IN INTERVENING IN THE SAID CASE;  WHEREAS, MOREOVER, THE SAID INTEREST IS NOT CONTESTED BY THE PARTIES TO THE ORIGINAL CASE;  
Operative part
THE COURT  COMPOSED OF : A . M . DONNER, PRESIDENT, CH . L . HAMMES AND N . CATALANO ( PRESIDENTS OF CHAMBERS ), O . RIESE, L . DELVAUX, J . RUEFF ( RAPPORTEUR ), AND R . ROSSI, JUDGES,  ADVOCATE-GENERAL : K . ROEMER  REGISTRAR : A . VAN HOUTTE  HEREBY ORDERS :  . THAT THE LAND OF NORTH RHINE-WESTPHALIA BE ALLOWED TO INTERVENE;  . THAT COPIES OF ALL PROCEDURAL DOCUMENTS BE SERVED BY THE REGISTRAR ON THE INTERVENER;  . THAT THE COSTS BE RESERVED .
