++++IN JOINED CASES AND 3/COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN ECONOMIC COMMUNITY  V  . GOVERNMENT OF THE GRAND DUCHY OF LUXEMBOURG  AND  . GOVERNMENT OF THE KINGDOM OF BELGIUM  
Subject of the case
THE COURT  COMPOSED OF : A . M . DONNER, PRESIDENT, L . DELVAUX AND R . ROSSI ( PRESIDENTS OF CHAMBERS ), O . RIESE, CH . L . HAMMES, A . TRABUCCHI AND R . LECOURT ( RAPPORTEUR ), JUDGES,  ADVOCATE GENERAL : K . ROEMER  REGISTRAR : A . VAN HOUTTE  
Grounds
WHEREAS, WITH THE OBJECT OF INVOKING THE ASSOCIATION AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE EEC AND GREECE AND ALSO THE PREAMBLE TO A BELGIAN LAW OF SEPTEMBER 1962, THE GOVERNMENT OF THE KINGDOM OF BELGIUM, BY AN APPLICATION DATED NOVEMBER 1962, HAS MADE A REQUEST FOR THE REOPENING OF THE ORAL PROCEDURE IN JOINED CASES AND 3/OF FEBRUARY 1962;  WHEREAS THE COURT CANNOT FIND IN THE MATTERS RAISED ANYTHING OF RELEVANCE WHICH WOULD JUSTIFY THE USE OF ITS POWERS UNDER ARTICLE OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE TO ORDER THE REOPENING OF THE ORAL PROCEDURE;  WHEREAS THE ASSOCIATION AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE EEC AND GREECE WAS FINALLY RATIFIED ( BY THE EXCHANGE OF INSTRUMENTS OF RATIFICATION ) BY THE GOVERNMENTS OF THE MEMBER STATES OF THE EEC, INCLUDING THE BELGIAN GOVERNMENT, ON AUGUST 1962, AS REPORTED IN THE 'MONITEUR BELGE' OF OCTOBER 1962, P . 9682;  WHEREAS THE BELGIAN LAW OF SEPTEMBER RECEIVED THE ASSENT OF THE KING OF THE BELGIANS ON THE SAME DATE AFTER IT HAD BEEN PUBLICLY DEBATED AND ADOPTED AT SITTINGS OF THE CHAMBER ON AND JULY AND OF THE SENATE ON JULY 1962, AS REPORTED IN THE 'MONITEUR BELGE' ON OCTOBER 1962, P . 9491;  WHEREAS THE SAID MATTERS WERE THUS KNOWN BEFORE OCTOBER 1962, THE DATE OF THE ORAL PROCEDURE, TO THE PARTY MAKING THE APPLICATION FOR THE REOPENING OF THE SAID PROCEDURE AND WHICH THEREFORE HAD THE TIME AND OPPORTUNITY TO ARGUE THEM AT THE HEARING;  WHEREAS THE SAID MATTERS WERE THEREFORE FOR APPLYING ARTICLE OF THE RULES OF PROCEDURE;  
Operative part
THE COURT  HEREBY  . DECLARES THAT THERE ARE NO GROUNDS FOR ORDERING THE REOPENING OF THE ORAL PROCEDURE;  . ORDERS THE PARTY MAKING THE APPLICATION OF NOVEMBER ON THE PROCEDURAL ISSUE TO PAY THE COSTS INVOLVED IN THAT APPLICATION .
